Music Wizard is a web application intended to give you recommendations about songs and artists of your favorite genre.

The application enables to choose a music genre and get the artists and songs which are most recognizable with this genre.

The application also recommends you about new genres, songs and artists which are close to the genrers you like.

Our application is based on smart queries to our huge music database! All the information is taken from DBPedia.

The app was developed as part of Database systems course in Tel Aviv University. We hope you enjoy our site!
